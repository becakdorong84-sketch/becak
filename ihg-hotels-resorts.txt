<h2>IHG Hotel Brands</h2>
<p>IHG has 17 hotel brands in its portfolio of 5,500+ hotels. The MMS team has stayed at nearly every <a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>IHG hotel brand</strong></a>, from rinky-dink interstate hotels to aspirational five-star hotels, like the InterContinental London Park Lane.</p>
<p>Each brand caters to a slightly different audience. I’ll give an overview of each IHG rewards hotel brand and show you how easy it is to stay for free. Earning the points, you need to <a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>stay at IHG hotels</strong></a> is easy because certain rewards you can earn with the best travel credit cards can be converted into IHG points. So you don’t necessarily even need a hotel credit card to earn free nights.</p>
<p>Like other hotel chains, IHG has multiple brands to meet the needs of different types of guests. For example, business travelers might choose one brand for upscale amenities, like a hotel lounge or business center, and others traveling on an extended vacation might choose a brand with full kitchens in hotel rooms to save on dining expenses.</p>

<p><a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Holiday Inn Express</strong></a> is the largest brand within the IHG chain with nearly 2,500 hotels.</p>
<p>These hotels are typically simple in design and are great for a short-term stay if you want an affordable price without fancy amenities. In the U.S., these hotels also offer a complimentary buffet breakfast.</p>
<h3>Holiday Inn Resorts</h3>
<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11077" src="https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-scaled.jpg" alt="Holiday Inn Resorts" width="2560" height="1920" srcset="https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-scaled.jpg 2560w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-300x225.jpg 300w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-1024x768.jpg 1024w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-768x576.jpg 768w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-1536x1152.jpg 1536w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-2048x1536.jpg 2048w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-53x40.jpg 53w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-450x338.jpg 450w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-900x675.jpg 900w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-43x32.jpg 43w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-107x80.jpg 107w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Holiday-Inn-Resorts-180x135.jpg 180w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px" /></p>

<p>Consider staying at a <a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Holiday Inn Resort hotel</strong></a> when traveling to popular tourist destinations. Within this brand, you’ll find all-inclusive hotels in destinations like Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, and Montego Bay in Jamaica. This brand offers plenty of amenities, including kids’ clubs, swimming pools, restaurants, and lounges, so you never have to leave the resort.</p>

<p><strong>IHG hotel brands</strong> have similar characteristics throughout their properties, but <a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Hotel Indigo</strong></a> is a chain of boutique hotels. Each location has a unique design, which is meant to reflect the character and history of the locale. For example, what you see and experience at the Hotel Indigo Nashville might be much different than at the Hotel Indigo Los Angeles Downtown. It’s a good escape from cookie-cutter hotels.</p>

The hotel incorporates Chinese hospitality into just about every aspect of the hotel, “with particular emphasis on the Chinese values of etiquette, rejuvenation in nature, recognition of status and enabling spaces,” according to the hotel website.</p>

These hotels usually have fantastic on-site restaurants and spas. There is even a separate level of IHG status called InterContinental Ambassador status just for InterContinental hotels. You can’t earn this status, instead, you have to pay $200 or 40,000 IHG points for a one-year membership.</p>

They’ve got 70+ hotels across the world and they’re actually worth checking out. As the name suggests, they’ve got award-winning dining — they even host a wine hour.</p>
<p>It sounds like the perfect ecosystem for hemp-sandaled hipsters, but it’s actually one of our favorite IHG brands, and staying at Kimpton hotels can be one of the best uses to use IHG points.</p>

In an annoying way. Beyond that, I can’t distill a proper brand identity from the website’s almost comical verbosity. <strong>The Regent Hotels</strong> currently are in six cities, including Beijing, Berlin, Porto Montenegro, and Singapore.</p>

<p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11071" src="https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Six-Senses-Hotels-Resorts.jpg" alt="Six Senses Hotels &amp; Resorts" width="600" height="300" srcset="https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Six-Senses-Hotels-Resorts.jpg 600w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Six-Senses-Hotels-Resorts-300x150.jpg 300w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Six-Senses-Hotels-Resorts-80x40.jpg 80w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Six-Senses-Hotels-Resorts-64x32.jpg 64w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Six-Senses-Hotels-Resorts-160x80.jpg 160w, https://scoopbiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Six-Senses-Hotels-Resorts-270x135.jpg 270w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></p>

These hotels look like top-tier properties within the IHG family. You can stay in prime areas like Fiji, Portugal, Seychelles, Maldives, Singapore, or Bali.</p>
<p>IHG has been introducing points bookings for Sixth Senses properties and you can now use your points at some locations, like the Sixth Senses hotels in Singapore, as reader Jordan C. has pointed out.</p>

<p>The chain is also pet-friendly and includes guest laundry and fitness facilities.</p>

<h2>Earn IHG points for free stays</h2>
<p>The IHG® Rewards Club Premier Credit Card currently has a bonus of 140,000 bonus points after you spend $3,000 on purchases in the first three months of account opening. The card has a $99 annual fee and each year you keep the card you’ll receive an anniversary night award valid at <a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>IHG Hotels &amp; Resorts</strong></a> that cost 40,000 points or less. This card also has the fourth-night award benefit and on award stays of 3+ nights, the fourth night won’t cost any points. In addition, you’ll get IHG Platinum status just for having the card.</p>
<p>You can also transfer Chase Ultimate Rewards points to IHG at a 1:1 ratio if you have any of these Chase credit cards:</p>
<ul>
<li>Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card</li>
<li>Chase Sapphire Reserve®</li>
<li>Ink Business Preferred Credit Card</li>
</ul>
<p>Just note that Chase will not approve you for a new card if you’ve opened five or more cards from any bank (not counting certain business credit cards) in the past 24 months.</p>
<h2>Bottom line</h2>
<p>You can book free stays at most of IHG’s 17 hotel brands. If you’re planning an extended-stay vacation, you might consider <strong>Candlewood Suites</strong> or <strong>Staybridge Suites</strong> because rooms include full kitchens. Or, for an upscale <a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>stay at a luxurious hotel</strong></a>, you’ll do better at <strong>InterContinental Hotels &amp; Resorts</strong> or <strong>Kimpton Hotels &amp; Restaurants</strong>. You can even book all-inclusive stays at <strong>Holiday Inn Resort</strong> brand hotels.</p>
<p>Let us know if you’ve got a <a href="https://fxo.co/F6NZ" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>favorite IHG hotel brand</strong></a>.</p>
